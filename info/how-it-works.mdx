After you install Reasonlayer, we route and secure all outbound traffic on the machine through an on-device proxy.

### What the Proxy Does

1. **Logs all requests**: Every request is logged for your own auditing purposes.
2. **Injects credentials**: We replace `rlcred_*` hashes with real credentials at request time, while respecting your allowed domain rules.
3. **Applies your rules**: Allowlist and blocklist rules are enforced before requests go out.

### What This Protects Against

Reasonlayer protects your agents from several threats: credential-stealing skills are blocked, malicious downloads and processes are blocked and logged in your audit log (alerting is coming soon), and accidental credential leaks to untrusted domains are prevented.

Reasonlayer also simplifies agent lifecycle management: you can onboard new agents with all your credentials preconfigured, and offboard them just as easily, keeping both setup and teardown fast and secure.
