To connect your agent to chat apps like WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, Slack, and more, follow the guides in [OpenClaw](https://docs.openclaw.ai/channels).
The supported chat channels for OpenClaw are Whatsapp, Telegram, Discord, Slack, Google Chat, Mattermost, Signal, iMessage (via Bluebubbles), Microsoft Teams, Twitch, IRC, Feishu, LINE, NextCloud Talk, Matrix, Nostr, Tlon, Zalo, Zalo Personal, Webchat.

You can access the terminal and filesystem in the code tab and terminal tab to implement the guides.
