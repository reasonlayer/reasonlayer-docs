## Before you begin

You'll need a Reasonlayer account and access to the [dashboard](https://app.reasonlayer.com). If you plan to automate work in another tool (like HubSpot or Gmail), have those credentials handy - see [Managing credentials](/connect/managing-credentials) and [Integrations](/connect/integrations).
